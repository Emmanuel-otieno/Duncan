Who I am

DUNCAN GASHIE “Music has been my first language”

“Rhythm and beat has kept my heart running since I was born.” Listed as one of the best drummers in Kenya by Drummer circle Kenya, Duncan takes pride in stating that He is a self-taught professional Drummer. Well as hard as that may sound, it is the main reason He decided to become a Drums teacher. He taught Drums in Melodica Music School for 2yrs and later decided to do freelance teaching as he gets more time in being a performing artist. He became a topper in Trinity College London and performed in their Annual concert in UAE 2018.
Duncan has played for well-known musicians in Kenya and International. He has been part of big bands i.e Tunedem band, Jazz loft band etc . Some of the Big Artists he has played for are Mutinda Award winning artist , Turbulence, The Late Oliver MtukunzI, Nazizi, Mercy Masika, Sarah K, Christafari to name but a few. He has also toured Korea and performed in concerts and conferences, he has been part of Festivals in Dubai and performed with Jive waves band, Unforgiven soul band, Drum neon etc.Duncan is Currently working with the Great EXPO2020 as a Busker with The Fridge
Duncan loves all genre of music and believes that music is the only language understood in the whole world and can unite all of us to be one.
